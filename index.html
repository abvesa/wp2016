This is a website that you can adjust your background color by your own.

1. Scroll your mouse wheel up and down over the RED, GREEN, BLUE buttons to find your favorate color.

2. Click on the combine color shows on the right button to change your background color.

3. OOPS, Be careful the color will invert when you hover it.

4. You may also scroll your mousewheel over the right button to randomly get colors.

5. At last, just have some fun !!!
